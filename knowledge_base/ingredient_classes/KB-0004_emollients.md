# Summary

Emollients are ingredients that soften, smooth, and improve the feel of the skin surface.

They help reduce roughness and improve skin texture by filling gaps between surface skin cells.

# Common Examples

- Squalane
- Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride
- Various plant oils
- Fatty alcohols
- Some silicones

# Why It Matters

Emollients may help:

- Improve skin softness
- Improve skin smoothness
- Reduce rough texture
- Improve moisturizer performance

# Common Misconceptions

## "All oils are occlusives."

False.

Many oils function primarily as emollients.
